Rendang Ayam

Description

The Chicken Rendang recipe is really delicious, the sauce is very fragrant because it uses fresh spices, finely ground and stir-fried until perfectly cooked. Slices of turmeric leaves and kaffir lime leaves add more flavor to this rendang.

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken (large size)
  • 1 spoon of ground chili
  • 1 large bowl of thick coconut milk
  • 1/2 small bowl of tamarind ice
  • 3 tablespoons crackling
  • 2 leaves of turmeric
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • salt and sugar to taste
  • 1 large bowl oil, for sauteing
    • Ingredients for stir-fried spices:
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 3 star anise flowers
  • 5 cardamom seeds
    • Ingredients for fine grinding:
  • 1 large onion
  • 8 shallots
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 inches of ginger
  • 3 pieces of live turmeric
  • 2 inches galangal
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass

Steps

  1. Clean the chicken and drain.
  2. Add in the chopped mushrooms and crank up the heat.
  3. Heat the oil and stir-fry the stir-fry spices, finely ground ingredients and ground chilies. Stir slowly and cook until oil breaks and crisp. The color of the stir-fry will turn slightly dark.
  4. Add the chicken and let it cook for 5-10 minutes. The chicken water will come out a little during this process.
  5. Add thick coconut milk and tamarind water. Stir gently over medium heat until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add crackling, turmeric leaves and kaffir lime leaves. Season with salt and sugar to taste.
  7. Sprinkle with sliced ​​turmeric leaves as decoration.
