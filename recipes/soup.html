Mushroom Soup Recipe

Description

A simple classic soup that can be enjoyed any time of year. You wonâ€™t be disappointed with the most comforting soup recipe there is.

Ingredients

  • 20g Butter
  • 20g Flour
  • 1 Large onion, finely diced
  • 2 Garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 White wine stockpot (Optional)
  • 600g Flat capped mushrooms, roughly chopped
  • 1Litre Chicken or veg stock stock
  • 200ml low fat milk

Steps

  1. In a large casserole pot, add the butter, onions and garlic and cook over a very low heat for 10 minutes, or until the onions are very soft.
  2. Add in the chopped mushrooms and crank up the heat.
  3. Cook for 5 minutes until nice and soft. Turn down the heat, add the flour and stir for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add in the stock, milk and white wine stockpot if using.
  5. Bring to a simmer and once you are happy with the consistency, blitz in a food processor or blender.
  6. Add a little dash of low fat cream, or truffle oil, and some fresh herbs like thyme or snipped chives if desired. Serve with crusty bread.
